Welcome to Club of Hope

Club of Hope is an NGO run by an independent group of like-minded individuals, providing aid to the children suffering from Thalassaemia & early detection of cancer, providing education to the girl child and help to old & unattended elders.

Club of Hope endeavors to give one glimmer of Hope to all those who believe, to create a society of people, where you dare to dream and have the courage to go & achieve.

About Club of Hope

The club endeavours to touch the lives of the few millions of children in fulfilling their dreams for education, the aged who have been left to tend for themselves and provide medical help & facilities to those who are suffering from Thalassaemia.

For every single life of sorrow & despair there is equivalent love & happiness in this world waiting to be shared. The club of Hope in its endeavour to make dreams come true & ensure Hope remains alive in thousands of those in need would like to extend its support in whatever small contribution possible.
